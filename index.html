Here's a timeline of APJ Abdul Kalam

  • 1931-Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India,
  • 1955 -Graduated from Madras Institute of Technology with a degree in aerospace engineering.
  • 1958-joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
  • 1980-Became the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle(SLV-III).
  • 1997-Had been honoured with a Bharat Ratna Award.
  • 2002-Became the 11th president of India.
  • 2003-Kalam supported the need of Uniform Civil Code in India.
  • 2012-He declined to contest the 2012 presidential poll.
  • 2012-Launched a programme for the youth of India called the 'What Can I Give Movement'
  • 2015-was confirmed dead due to sudden cardiac arrest
